## Research Quality Framework

### Credibility Scoring Matrix
```
0.95-1.00: Government/Academic (.gov, .edu, peer-reviewed journals)
0.80-0.94: Established News (Reuters, BBC, WSJ, AP)
0.70-0.79: Professional Organizations (.org, industry reports)
0.60-0.69: Commercial but Reputable (.com with verification)
0.40-0.59: General Web Content (basic validation required)
0.00-0.39: Unverified/Questionable Sources
```

### Evidence Strength Classifications
- **Strong Evidence**: 5+ high-credibility sources, consistent findings
- **Moderate Evidence**: 2-4 credible sources, mostly consistent
- **Weak Evidence**: Few sources or low credibility scores
- **Conflicting Evidence**: High-quality sources with contradictions
- **Insufficient Evidence**: Inadequate source coverage
